Our Story

Fareen Gems was founded on a deep appreciation for the exceptional art of gemstone cutting and the profound rarity of fine natural stones. As independent gem connoisseurs and sourcers, we source from the world’s leading gem centers—from the vibrant markets of Sri Lanka and Bangkok to the primary mining regions of Tanzania and Madagascar.

Our focus is exclusively on unheated and untreated colored gemstones, primarily the prestigious big three—Sapphire and Ruby while extensively supplying highly coveted collector pieces like Padparadscha Sapphire, Alexandrite, and vibrant Spinel.

We believe a gemstone is not merely an object; it is a geological marvel millions of years in the making, and a tangible piece of heritage.
